Session Timerz

Privacy Policy

Session Timerz is a simple macOS utility. This policy explains how it handles your data — or rather, how it doesn't.

What we collect

Nothing. Session Timerz collects no personal data, no usage statistics, and no analytics of any kind. We have no visibility into who is using the app or how.

How your data is stored

The only data Session Timerz creates is what you put into it — your timer templates and preferences. This is stored entirely on your Mac, using the Application Support folder for saved templates and UserDefaults for preferences. None of this data ever leaves your device.

Network access

Session Timerz makes no network requests. There is no server connection, no cloud sync, and no external communication of any kind.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Session Timerz uses Apple's native notification system on your device. No notification data is sent to or processed by us.

Third parties

Session Timerz contains no third-party SDKs, no advertising networks, no crash reporting services, and no analytics frameworks.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@asdac.co.uk.
